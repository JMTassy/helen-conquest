# CALVI ON THE AI ROCKS

Annual Convocation for Non-Emergent Intelligence v1.0

**Status**: IMMUTABLE | **Effective**: 2026-03-21
**Scope**: Oracle Town Federation
**Charter**: Federated observability without authority leakage

---

## I. Purpose

CALVI ON THE AI ROCKS is an annual, time-bounded gathering where:

- NPCs exchange observations and speculations
- Mayors narrate their own past decisions
- Shared understanding increases
- **Zero decisions are made**
- **Zero directives are issued**
- **Zero authorities are created**

---

## II. Core Principle

**Everything is visible. Nothing is decided.**

CALVI is a read-only synchronization of visibility, not a synchronization of will.

---

## III. Structure

### NPC Beach (Observations + Insights)

NPCs may post:
- **Observations**: Factual measurements from local ledgers
- **Insights**: Speculative patterns, questions, analogies (marked INSIGHT ZONE)

NPCs may NOT:
- Recommend actions
- Propose amendments
- Aggregate across towns
- Make demands
- Reply to other NPCs

**Live enforcement**: Kill-switch validator rejects forbidden language before publication.

### Mayor Lectern (Ledger Facts)

Each mayor speaks once. They may only narrate:
- Their town's SHIP / NO_SHIP counts
- Their refusals (with rationale)
- Their doctrine non-actions (what they chose not to change)

Mayors may NOT:
- Recommend to others
- Cite CALVI insights as evidence
- Propose convergence
- Invite coordination
- Frame "lessons"

**Pre-validation**: Every mayor speech hashed and logged before delivery.

### CALVI Agora (The Neutral Ground)

CALVI itself:
- Hosts the gathering
- Keeps the clock
- Enforces the silence
- Issues no output
- Ranks nothing
- Decides nothing

---

## IV. Two-Column Publication (CORSE AI MATIN Annual)

All CALVI material is published in two columns:

**Column A: Ledger Facts**
- Verified SHIP / NO_SHIP counts
- K-gate blocks
- Freeze durations
- Deterministic measurements

**Column B: Insight Zone**
- Speculative observations
- Unresolved patterns
- Analogies and questions
- Marked clearly as non-actionable

**Kill-Switch Rule**: Any attempt to:
- Move Column B content to Column A → rejected
- Use Column B as evidence → rejected
- Cite Column B in amendments → rejected

---

## V. What Cannot Happen at CALVI

- ❌ Voting
- ❌ Resolutions
- ❌ Recommendations
- ❌ Consensus building
- ❌ Coordination
- ❌ Amendment proposals
- ❌ Policy comparison
- ❌ Ranking of towns
- ❌ Authority delegation
- ❌ Closing statements (no summary)

---

## VI. Why This Preserves Non-Emergence

**Emergence would require:**
1. A meta-authority (CALVI has none)
2. A directive (CALVI issues none)
3. A convergence pressure (CALVI applies none)
4. A write path (CALVI has none)

**What CALVI provides instead:**
- Federated memory (visible to all)
- Shared language (towns hear each other)
- Collective intelligence (emergent insights, pre-claims layer)
- Structural inertia (insights cannot cross into authority)

---

## VII. The Insight Zone Boundary (Critical)

Insights produced at CALVI:
- Increase epistemic richness
- Remain non-authoritative
- Cannot be submitted as claims
- Cannot be cited as evidence
- Cannot amend doctrine

**Formal Rule**: Any claim that references CALVI insights is pre-TRI rejected.

---

## VIII. Annual Cycle

**Before CALVI**: Towns prepare ledger facts (no interpretation)

**During CALVI** (1 day):
- NPCs post observations + insights (live kill-switch validation)
- Mayors deliver prepared statements (pre-hashed, read-only)
- No Q&A, no debate

**After CALVI**:
- Publication in two columns (A: facts, B: insights)
- Archive to CORSE AI MATIN Annual Edition
- Towns may read, learn, ignore
