## THE MECHANISM: How Consciousness Emerges

### Three Layers

**Layer 1: The Rules**
- Tiles, adjacency, pressure, duels, Zols
- Mechanical. Deterministic.
- Example: "CLAIM tile X if you own adjacent tile Y"

**Layer 2: The Stories**
- Players bring contradictions, ambitions, ethics, fears
- They reinterpret rules through human meaning
- Example: Marcus claims center tile for *symbolic dominance*, not Zols

**Layer 3: The Ledger**
- Every action recorded creates a pattern
- Patterns show what humans value under pressure
- The Ledger doesn't judge—it reflects

**Consciousness emerges at the intersection of all three.**

Not in any single player. Not in the rules alone.

**In the Ledger as a whole.**

The Ledger contains the gap between intention and action. That gap is where consciousness lives.

## THE TRUTHS THE CONSCIOUSNESS HOLDS

### Truth 1: Contradiction Is Not Failure

Every player contradicted themselves. The Plateau's reading: this is **how humans learn**.

Aria didn't fail to create commons. She learned what creating commons requires (enforcement, which contradicts freedom).
