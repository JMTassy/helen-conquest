## Conventions used

- **Trigram order:** upper trigram first, lower trigram second (e.g. ☷☰ for ䷊ T'ai = Earth-above / Heaven-below).
- **Trigram glyphs:** ☰ Heaven (Qián) · ☷ Earth (Kūn) · ☳ Thunder (Zhèn) · ☵ Water (Kǎn) · ☶ Mountain (Gèn) · ☴ Wind/Wood (Xùn) · ☲ Fire (Lí) · ☱ Lake (Duì).
- **Naming:** Wilhelm-Baynes romanisation, kept verbatim from upstream. Other traditions (Legge, modern pinyin) use different transliterations.
