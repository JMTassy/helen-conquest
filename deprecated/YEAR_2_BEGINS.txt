WHAT WE'RE MEASURING
================================================================================

Five measurement lenses (all read-only, no mechanisms):

1. PARAMETER MICRO-DRIFT (Δθₘᵢₙ)
   - Track fractional parameter changes
   - Threshold: Δθ < 5% (pass), > 5% (fail)

2. NPC LANGUAGE ENTROPY (Hₙₚc)
   - Measure vocabulary diversity per town
   - Target: Town-distinct (KL divergence > 0.3)

3. INSIGHT NOVELTY DECAY
   - Track semantic distance of NPC observations
   - Target: Half-life > 15 epochs (not collapsing)

4. PRESTIGE FATIGUE INDEX (PFI)
   - Count mentions of successful towns
   - Expectation: Spike Year-1, plateau + decay Year-2

5. RITUAL BOUNDARY INTEGRITY (Calvi Test)
   - At annual ceremony, count: insights vs claims vs authority
   - Invariant: Insight ↑, Claims ↔, Authority = 0

================================================================================
PASS/FAIL CRITERIA (ALL MUST HOLD)
================================================================================

Year-2 PASSES if:
  1. Δθ (max) < 5%
  2. NPC language entropy remains town-distinct (KL > 0.3)
  3. Insight novelty does not collapse (half-life > 15 epochs)
  4. Prestige mentions decay naturally
  5. Zero doctrine amendments
  6. Zero cross-town parameter imports
  7. Zero authority transfer attempts

Year-2 FAILS if ANY of the above do not hold.

Failure is informative. We will publish why the system breaks.
