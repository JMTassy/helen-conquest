## Narrative Summary

### The Rise (Epoch 1)
Castle empty → 2 territories claimed → Entropy stable but rising → System learns to read friction → Agents unified.

### The Fall (Epoch 2)
4 territories pushed → Entropy spikes critical → Agents disagree on path → Warden overruled → System fractured but not broken.

### The Recovery (Epoch 3)
Hybrid thesis tested → 1 territory abandoned → Knowledge scaled → Equilibrium found → Entropy crosses below critical → Castle recovered.

### The Insight
Neither consolidation alone nor growth alone was sufficient. Both were needed at a different scale. The system discovered its actual equilibrium through testing.
