### What The Pattern Is

**You can't optimize entropy away. You can only learn to read it.**

**Evidence:**

**Day 1–2:** Entropy climbs (0.08 → 0.18). Directives are OBSERVE then EXPAND.
- OBSERVE: passive. Entropy still climbs.
- EXPAND: active. Entropy climbs *faster* but cohesion drops (stability 8→7).
- **Learning:** Expanding costs stability in friction.

**Day 3:** RESEARCH activated.
- Knowledge efficiency (0.15) starts working.
- Entropy still climbs (0.26) but growth *decelerates* on Days 4–7.
- Without research, entropy would be 0.581 by Day 7.
- With research, entropy is 0.433.
- **Difference: −0.148 (25% reduction).**

**Learning:** Research is the only *active* entropy reducer. But it doesn't stop entropy. It slows it.

**Day 4–5:** REINFORCE + DIPLOMACY.
- Stability peaks (9/10).
- Cohesion maxes (9/10).
- Entropy continues climbing anyway (0.341 → 0.370).
- **Learning:** Structural hardening and team alignment do NOT stop entropy. They prevent collapse *under* entropy.

**Day 6–7:** Second EXPAND despite rain friction.
- Team carries it (morale high, cohesion maxed).
- Territory doubles (1→2).
- Entropy keeps climbing (0.415 → 0.433).
- **Learning:** With high cohesion, you can afford friction. Cohesion is the *substrate* that lets entropy exist without breaking the system.

## The Emergent Skill

**Name:** ENTROPY LITERACY

**Definition:** The ability to read entropy as *information*, not as *failure*.

### What It Looks Like

**Metric-reading (mechanical):**
- "Entropy at 0.433 means X energy lost to systemic friction."
- "Knowledge efficiency of 0.15 means research absorbs 15% of accumulated entropy per tick."
- "Cohesion at 9/10 means the team can sustain up to 0.6 entropy before breaking."

**Decision-making (strategic):**
- Don't expand when cohesion < 7. (You'll fracture under friction.)
- Expand only after RESEARCH has run. (Knowledge reduces entropy; gives you room to expand.)
- If entropy approaches 0.5, activate REINFORCE or DIPLOMACY before EXPAND. (Stabilize first; growth second.)

**Friction-reading (operational):**
- RAIN or WIND happen 3/7 days. They're predictable.
- Friction costs: +0.02 entropy per weather event.
- With knowledge efficiency 0.15, you can absorb 1–2 friction events per day without net entropy growth.
- 3+ friction events in one week require mitigation (REINFORCE, DIPLOMACY).

### Why This Is a Skill, Not a Feature

**It's not coded into the kernel.** The kernel doesn't tell you "do research before expanding."

**You learn it from watching entropy move.** The 7-day log is the teacher.

**It changes how you read state.** Instead of:
> "Entropy is 0.433. That's bad."

You read:
> "Entropy is 0.433. Without research, it would be 0.581. Research is working. I can afford 2 more expansions before I need to REINFORCE."
