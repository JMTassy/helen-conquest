## KEY INNOVATIONS

### 1. Autonomy Within Constitution
Most systems offer either:
- Option A: Complete freedom (no constraints) → chaos
- Option B: Complete control (all constraints) → rigidity

This offers:
- Option C: Maximum freedom within immutable constitution → sustainable autonomy

### 2. Authority Separation Enforced Mechanically
Not by saying "don't do X"
But by making "X" physically impossible:
- Voting cannot happen (not in the code)
- Authority cannot aggregate (lanes return metrics, not decisions)
- Constraints cannot be suspended (they're in the kernel)

### 3. Emergence Measured, Not Narrated
Instead of: "The town naturally became more creative"
We measure: "Proposal novelty increased from 5.2 to 7.8 (p < 0.01), constrained by these relaxations"
