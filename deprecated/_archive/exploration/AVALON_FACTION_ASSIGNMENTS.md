## FACTION ASSIGNMENT LOGIC

### WARDEN (Security/Power) → ✝️ TEMPLAR

**Why:**
- Warden's core: "Hold the perimeter. Defend structure. Don't break formation."
- Templar's core: Order, crystallization, structural integrity
- Alignment: 100% (discipline, fortress doctrine, resistance to chaos)

**Mechanical Fit:**
- Warden proposes REINFORCE (matches Templar passive: +DEFENSE in fortress)
- Warden fears chaos/entropy (matches Templar weakness: −1 POWER during chaos spikes)
- Warden cannot initiate EXPAND if STABILITY < 2 (matches Templar rule: frozen posture in crisis)

**Alchemical State Preference:**
- Primary: 🜃 SALT (Crystallization/Defense) — "Hold the line"
- Fallback: 🏰 Fortress positioning (passive)

**Status in Epoch 3:** Vindicated (Fortress thesis proven correct). Starts at calm, professional.

---

### ARCHIVIST (Knowledge/Optimization) → 🌹 ROSICRUCIAN

**Why:**
- Archivist's core: "Gather data. Understand systems. Find patterns. Transmute into knowledge."
- Rosicrucian's core: Research, alchemy, strategic transformation
- Alignment: 100% (knowledge multiplication, transmutation of resources into understanding)

**Mechanical Fit:**
- Archivist proposes RESEARCH (matches Rosicrucian passive: +KNOWLEDGE mult per research)
- Archivist can CONVERT territories into knowledge tokens (matches Rosicrucian ability)
- Archivist is fragile in war (matches Rosicrucian weakness: −1 POWER during conflict)

**Alchemical State Preference:**
- Primary: 🜂 MERCURY (Mobility/Transition) — "Flow between domains. Adapt."
- Secondary: 🜄 AQUA REGIA (Transmutation) — "Convert raw territory → refined knowledge"

**Status in Epoch 3:** Analytical observer. Watches both theses (Warden vs Steward). Provides data without judging.

---

### STEWARD (Cohesion/Diplomacy) → 🌀 CHAOS

**Why:**
- Steward's core: "Build alliances. Take calculated risks. Move fast. Embrace uncertainty."
- Chaos's core: Volatility, reality-bending, high variance, glitch-driven effects
- Alignment: 90% (risk-taking, non-linear problem solving, but NOT destructive)

**Mechanical Fit:**
- Steward proposes DIPLOMACY + EXPAND (matches Chaos: +EXPAND action, reality-bending alliances)
- Steward sees growth as possible despite entropy (matches Chaos: can surge despite instability)
- Steward's morale-driving proposals feel chaotic to Warden (matches Chaos vs Templar friction)

**Alchemical State Preference:**
- Primary: 🜁 SULFUR (Volatility/Offense) — "Heat, aggression, forward motion"
- Secondary: 🜍 PHILOSOPHER'S STONE (rare equilibrium) — "Achieved when all factions align"

**Status in Epoch 3:** Embattled but vindicated by Hybrid Thesis. Proves growth IS possible (at 3 territories + 0.225 knowledge).

**Caveat:** Steward is not "destructive chaos" (no mindless IGNITE). Steward's chaos is *purposeful volatility* — risk-taking in service of cohesion. This is the key distinction.
