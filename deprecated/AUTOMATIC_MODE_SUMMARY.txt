   Finding: System prioritizes safety over functionality

   Impact: Faster cycles

   Benefit: 50%+ faster execution for repeated patterns
   Effort: Low
   Impact: Speed up production workloads

   Optimization: Archive old entries, keep recent ones hot
   Benefit: Reduce storage from TB (years) to GB
   Effort: Medium
   Impact: Long-term scalability
