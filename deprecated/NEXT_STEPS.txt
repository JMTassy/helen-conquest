Your ORACLE TOWN evolves from:

  1. Daily Digest (automatic insights every 24h)

  The town measures its own accuracy and improves continuously.
  District thresholds adjust automatically based on outcomes.

  - Temporal patterns (when do things cluster?)

  - Accuracy signals (which districts predict best?)
