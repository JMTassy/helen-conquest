## PART II: THE CALVI DOCTRINE

### 1. Receipt-First Discipline

**Principle**: No claim without ledger. No promise without pin.

Every capability announcement from Calvi Academy will be accompanied by:
- **WUL Token Tree** (machine-checkable claim structure)
- **Tribunal Bundle** (required obligations with severity levels)
- **Attestations Ledger** (who verified what, when, how)
- **Mayor Decision Record** (deterministic SHIP/NO_SHIP with reason codes)

We do not ask the world to trust us. We give the world receipts to verify us.

### 2. The Guardian Mode

**Calvi is not a factory. Calvi is a guardian.**

We train operators for:
- **Multi-environment autonomy** (UAV/USV/AUV in Mediterranean complexity)
- **Ethical decision protocols** (when to override, when to ground, when to abort)
- **Scientific stewardship** (FAIR data, reproducible research, open validation)

Our graduates do not merely pilot drones. They guard the boundary between capability and wisdom.

### 3. The Mediterranean Advantage

**Geography is destiny. We embrace it.**

- **STARESO Research Station**: 50+ years of marine science, now integrated with robotic observation platforms
- **Calvi Bay**: Natural laboratory for autonomous maritime systems (currents, visibility gradients, marine traffic)

- **European Regulatory Crossroads**: French legal framework + EU harmonization + Mediterranean partnership networks

What Silicon Valley simulates, we validate in the real world.

## APPENDICES

### Appendix A: Glossary

- **Receipt-First Governance**: Decision framework where outcomes are determined by validated artifacts (receipts), not narrative claims
- **WUL Token Tree**: Machine-checkable claim structure using bounded symbolic kernel (no free text)
- **Tribunal Bundle**: Set of required obligations with severity levels (HARD/SOFT) that must be satisfied for claim validation
- **Attestations Ledger**: Record of who verified which obligations, with timestamps and validation status
- **Mayor Decision**: Deterministic function: SHIP iff (kill_switches_pass AND receipt_gap == 0)
- **Guardian Mode**: Ethical training framework emphasizing when to override, ground, or abort autonomous operations
- **Jumeau Numérique**: Digital twin — high-fidelity simulation environment for pre-deployment testing
- **FAIR Data**: Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable — scientific data standards
