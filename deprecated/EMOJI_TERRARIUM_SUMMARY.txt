WHAT IS THIS?
================================================================================

A minimal, joyful 3-agent territorial simulation with:

✅ Emoji Avatars (😊 😠 😌) that feel alive and expressive
✅ EMOWUL Emotional Model (Valence, Arousal, Dominance) driving behavior
✅ Real-time mood visualization (emoji + PAD bars)
✅ Dynamic combat influenced by emotional state
✅ Emotional arcs (agents change mood based on victories/defeats)

Think: Three smiley faces fighting over territory, with changing emotions
       visible every turn.

================================================================================
EMOWUL EMOTIONAL MODEL
================================================================================

Three dimensions of emotion:

VALENCE (0.0 sad → 1.0 happy)
  - Represents overall happiness/mood
  - Increases with victories, expansion
  - Decreases with defeats
  - Bars: V:████░ (80% happy) to V:█░░░░ (20% happy)

AROUSAL (0.0 sleepy → 1.0 excited)
  - Represents activation/energy level
  - Increases during conflict, threats
  - Decreases during fortification, rest
  - Bars: A:█████ (100% excited) to A:░░░░░ (0% excited)

DOMINANCE (0.0 submissive → 1.0 dominant)
  - Represents control/confidence
  - Increases with victories
  - Decreases with defeats
  - Influences combat power
  - Bars: D:█████ (100% dominant) to D:░░░░░ (0% dominant)

Example Display:
  😊 SUNNY | 😊 EUPHORIC | V:████░ A:███░░ D:██░░░

Translation:
  - Happy face (😊) shows positive valence
  - EUPHORIC = happy + excited (high V + high A)
  - V:████░ = 80% valence (happy)
  - A:███░░ = 60% arousal (moderately excited)
  - D:██░░░ = 40% dominance (somewhat submissive)

================================================================================
GAME MECHANICS
================================================================================

Grid: 3×3 (9 tiles)
Victory: Control ≥5 tiles (majority)
Turns: 20 max
Conflicts: 20% per adjacent pair per turn

ACTIONS BY PERSONALITY:

😊 SUNNY:  Expand (claim unclaimed adjacent tiles)
😠 STORM:  Attack (seek adjacent enemy tiles to conquer)
😌 PEACE:  Fortify (increase defense on owned tiles)

COMBAT MATH:
  Attacker Power = Power + (Dominance × 3) + d3
  Defender Power = Stability + (1 - Arousal) × 2 + d3
  
  Winner takes tile and updates emotions

EXAMPLE CONFLICT:
  Turn 1: 😠 STORM attacks 😊 SUNNY
  - STORM: Power=5 + (0.7×3) + 2 = 9.1
  - SUNNY: Stability=5 + (0.6) + 1 = 5.6
  - STORM wins → Takes tile, becomes euphoric
  - SUNNY loses → Becomes sad
