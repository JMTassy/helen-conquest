---

## THE DECISION

**Week 3 = Silence Week (No mayor intervention, no new constraints, no announcement).**

---

## WHY SILENCE WORKS HERE

### What Silence Reveals

Without environmental pressure or external validation:
- ✅ True output emerges (not reaction to constraint)
- ✅ Collaboration reveals genuine alignment (not forced by scarcity)
- ✅ Identity stabilizes (not reactive to feedback)
- ✅ Fatigue appears (or doesn't — this is data)
- ✅ Self-organization shows its ceiling

### What Silence Prevents

- ❌ Artificial momentum from governance novelty
- ❌ Premature systematization (too early to know what to measure)
- ❌ Authority creep (mayor becomes visible = authority begins)
- ❌ Narrative capture (silence resists mythmaking)

---

## WEEK 3 OPERATIONAL STRUCTURE

### Role of Mayor: OBSERVATIONAL ONLY

**DO**:
- ✅ Log all artifacts (date, district, type, quality signal)
- ✅ Track collaboration patterns (who mentions whom? who merges work?)
- ✅ Note energy (is output sustaining or declining?)
- ✅ Identify friction (where do visions conflict?)
- ✅ Record silence (gaps are data)

**DON'T**:
- ❌ Respond to requests for feedback
- ❌ Introduce constraints (let Week 2 constraints expire naturally)
- ❌ Announce anything (silence includes silent interface)
- ❌ Judge quality (artifacts speak, don't evaluate)
- ❌ Coordinate between districts (let them find each other or not)

**Communication Method**: Zero public. Silent ledger only.

---

## WHAT TO MEASURE (No publication, only logging)

### Artifact Flow

```
Track per district:
  - Output count (daily)
  - Output type (new vs. refinement vs. variation)
  - Collaboration signals (explicit mentions, merges, references)
  - Energy quality (signs of fatigue vs. momentum)
```

### Collaboration Emergence

```
Map:
  - Who reads whose work
  - Who builds on previous artifacts
  - Who challenges or contradicts
  - Who stays isolated
  - Who acts as bridge between districts
```

### Identity Coherence

```
Per district:
  - Core concept from Week 1 → still visible?
  - Divergence increasing or stabilizing?
  - Voice becoming sharper or blurrier?
  - Scope creeping or holding?
```

### Fatigue Signals

```
Watch for:
  - Declining output frequency
  - Shorter artifacts (effort reduction)
  - Recycled ideas (ideation exhaustion)
  - Reduced collaboration (individuals retreat)
  - Silent periods (days with no output)
```

### Emergence Signals

```
Watch for:
  - New roles forming (unplanned)
  - Cross-district references
  - Unplanned constraints appearing (self-imposed)
  - Patterns forming without instruction
  - Direction clarifying without declaration
```

## DECISION TREE (After Week 3 Silence)

### If Signal is Strong:
**Observation: Artifacts cohere, collaboration emerges, identity clarifies, energy sustains**

→ **Next move**: Introduce 1 formal test (e.g., "Can these artifacts survive external critique?")

### If Signal is Mixed:
**Observation: Some districts strong, some fading; collaboration spotty; identity fractured**

→ **Next move**: Surgical intervention in weak districts (not governance, just attention)

### If Signal is Weak:
**Observation: Output declining, isolation increasing, identity blurring, fatigue visible**

→ **Next move**: Hard cut. Redesign or abort. No rescue attempts.

### If Signal is Incoherent:
**Observation: Artifacts conflict, no collaboration, multiple visions, chaos**

→ **Next move**: Introduce hard constraint (force consolidation or divergence into separate towns)
