# CONQUEST TOWN: Operating Manual
**How to run Weeks 1–3 (and beyond)**

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

**The town reveals itself through artifacts, not discussion.**

Authority should be invisible until it must act.

---

## WEEK 1: EXPANSION (No constraints, pure production)

### Mayor's Job
- Set 5 district names and rough purpose
- Announce publicly (once)
- Step back

### Archetype's Job
- Create artifacts
- Publish freely
- Explore widely

### What to Expect
- High output volume
- Variable quality
- Wide exploration
- Visible identity emerging

### Success Signal
- Artifacts appear in all 5 districts
- Some artifacts reference other districts
- Creators seem energized
- Direction begins forming (not declared)

---

## WEEK 2: CONSTRAINT (One environmental adjustment)

### Mayor's Job
- Introduce ONE scarcity: "2 artifacts per district max"
- Announce clearly (once)
- Do not explain rationale
- Observe

### Archetype's Job
- Choose carefully (forced prioritization)
- Refine over quantity
- Debate internally (conflicts visible)
- Self-organize around limits

### What to Expect
- Output quality increases
- Collaboration increases (merging work)
- Conflict surfaces (identity crises visible)
- Energy shifts from expansion to direction

### Success Signal
- Creators stay despite constraint
- Quality visibly improves
- Collaboration appears
- Districts start differentiating

---

## WEEK 3: SILENCE (Zero mayor intervention)

### Mayor's Job
- Silent observation only
- Log everything (private ledger)
- Answer zero questions
- Take zero action

### Archetype's Job
- Continue production (or don't)
- Self-organize
- Reveal true motivation
- Show fatigue or strength

### What to Expect
- Output stabilizes or declines
- Collaboration deepens or reveals isolation
- Identity clarifies or blurs
- Direction becomes undeniable or chaos appears

### Success Signal
- Signal coherence measurable
- Collaboration map visible
- Identity locked or fractured
- Fatigue or momentum undeniable

---

## BEYOND WEEK 3

After assessment:

**SCALE** → Introduce external testing (artifact review by non-creators)
**PIVOT** → Intervene surgically in weak districts
**CONSOLIDATE** → Cut low-signal districts, double down on strong ones
**ABORT** → Restart with new constraints

---

## CRITICAL RULES

### For Mayor
1. **Announce once, act once** — No constant communication
2. **Constraints, not instructions** — Set environment, not outcomes
3. **Silence breaks is death** — Once you start explaining, authority begins
4. **Artifact focus, not process** — Judge by output, not discussion
5. **No rescue attempts** — If energy dies, let it die

### For Archetypes
1. **Output is participation** — Only artifacts count
2. **Quality improves under pressure** — Don't pity them
3. **Collaboration emerges naturally** — Don't force it
4. **Conflict clarifies identity** — Don't smooth it over
5. **Fatigue is honest** — Don't hide it

### For Everyone
1. **No mythology** — Call what you see
2. **No cheerleading** — Data, not motivation
3. **No consensus** — Majority doesn't win; signal does
4. **No slowness** — Decisions binary, made fast
5. **No half-measures** — Silence means silence

---

## WHAT TO TRACK (Silent Ledger)

**Per artifact**:
- Timestamp (exact)
- District
- Title
- Type (new / refinement / variation / conflict)
- Collaboration signals (explicit mentions, merges, references)
- Energy signal (quality, effort level)
- Notes (patterns observed)

**Per district (daily)**:
- Output count
- Collaboration in/out
- Energy level (visual assessment)
- Fatigue signals (if any)
- Direction (if clarifying)

**Aggregate (weekly)**:
- Total output vs. previous week
- Collaboration density (cross-district connections)
- Identity coherence (is each district recognizable?)
- Fatigue level (aggregate)
- Direction clarity (is the town pointing somewhere?)

---

## DECISION POINTS (The Only Times to Act)

1. **End of Week 1**: Did signal appear in all districts?
   - **YES** → proceed to Week 2
   - **NO** → redesign districts, restart

2. **End of Week 2**: Did constraint improve quality and collaboration?
   - **YES** → proceed to Week 3 silence
   - **NO** → adjust constraint or abort

3. **End of Week 3**: Is signal strong enough to continue?
   - **YES** → scale or pivot based on assessment
   - **NO** → abort, redesign, restart

---

## THE SILENCE RULE (Most Important)

**Week 3 is non-negotiable silence.**

If you break it (answer a question, offer feedback, introduce constraint):
- The experiment becomes about your attention, not their signal
- Authority becomes visible
- Mythology replaces observation
- You've corrupted the data

**Duration**: 7 full days, zero communication.

**If you can't do it**, don't start Week 3.

---

## FAILURE MODES

### Mode 1: Premature Communication
**Symptom**: Mayor answers questions, explains rationale, provides feedback during silence
**Result**: Town becomes reactive to authority, signal obscured
**Fix**: Enforce silence strictly

### Mode 2: No Constraints
**Symptom**: Creators produce infinite artifacts with no pressure
**Result**: Output volume masks lack of direction
**Fix**: Introduce Week 2 constraint

### Mode 3: Infinite Constraints
**Symptom**: New rules every week, environmental chaos
**Result**: Creators optimize for rules, not truth
**Fix**: One constraint per week maximum

### Mode 4: No Assessment
**Symptom**: Silent Ledger kept but never analyzed
**Result**: You have data but no decision
**Fix**: Assessment template completed by Friday EOD Week 3

### Mode 5: Assessment Without Action
**Symptom**: Assessment completed but no next move decided
**Result**: Town stalls in limbo
**Fix**: Make binary decision (SCALE / PIVOT / CONSOLIDATE / ABORT)
