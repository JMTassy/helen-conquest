## Four Core Components

### 1. Personal Investment Doctrine

**Four Investment Classes:**
- **CLASS_I (Governable)** — Evidence exists, bounded risk, reversible
- **CLASS_II (Conditionally Governable)** — Partial evidence, validation delayed
- **CLASS_III (Speculative)** — Narrative-driven, irreversible, intuition-based
- **CLASS_IV (Existential)** — Identity-defining, cannot be optimized

**Acceptance Rate Law:**
- Target: 35-45% ACCEPT
- Below 30%: Over-submitting noise
- Above 50%: Under-filtering

**Refusal Doctrine:**
- A REJECT is a completed act of governance
- Do not argue with REJECTs
- Ask only: "What behavior did this prevent?"

**Override Protocol:**
- Allowed only for CLASS_III/IV
- Logged permanently with amount, reason, review date
- No retroactive justification

### 2. Doctrine Enforcer (Pre-TRI Gate)

Validates submissions **before** authority judgment:
- Rejects CLASS_III claims submitted as CLASS_I
- Detects narrative laundering ("I believe", "I feel")
- Enforces evidence requirements per class
- Makes structure mandatory, not advisory

**Result:** No escape path for soft language or wishful thinking

### 3. Override Ledger (Immutable Record)

Logs conscious speculations permanently:
- €700k LE TAR wall: "Review date 2027-01-31"
- All identity-defining decisions tracked
- Outcomes recorded when review date arrives
- Success rate computed (helps RiskAnalyzer)

### 4. NPC Framework (Autonomous Observers)

**Four Built-in NPCs:**

**AccuracyWatcher**
- Observes: REJECTs prevent X bad decisions
- Estimates cost of prevented failures
- Validates that refusal is working

**SpeculationTracker**
- Observes: Active identity-defining speculations
- Tracks toward review dates
- Alerts if timeline at risk

**PatternDetector**
- Observes: Shifts in acceptance/rejection rates
- Detects anomalies (rejection rate 59% vs 40%)
- Proposes investigation if needed

**RiskAnalyzer**
- Observes: Outcome of reviewed overrides
- Measures success rate of conscious bets
- Validates risk tolerance is appropriate

**Key Property:** NPCs have ZERO authority
- They only OBSERVE and PROPOSE
- Claims go through normal governance
- You retain single authority
