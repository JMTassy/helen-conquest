## The Oath

```

We choose collaboration under constraint.

We reject hidden authority.
Every claim must carry a receipt.

We reject narrative as proof.
Poetry may guide the surface;
only validated artifacts may alter the ledger.

We reject false certainty.
The unknown remains marked as unknown.

We reject unbounded intelligence.
Cognition may generate infinite possibilities;
governance may admit only what is typed, checked, and replayable.

We bind human and artificial intelligence through a single law:

NO RECEIPT → NO SHIP.

The rune is not the proof.
The symbol is not the law.
The ledger is the witness.
```
