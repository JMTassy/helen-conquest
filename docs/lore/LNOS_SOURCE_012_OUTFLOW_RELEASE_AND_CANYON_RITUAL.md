## 3. Mechanic — Outflow Release

**Purpose:** a **non-medical, fictional ritual mechanic** for clearing
burdened quest states. **Not a wellness practice.** **Not a psychiatric
intervention.** **Not a healing protocol.**

**Inputs:**
- magnetometer reading (`#011`)
- tone calibration (`#010`)
- breath timing
- posture gesture
- memory token selected by player

**Process:**
1. Player reaches an Outflow Point.
2. Magnetometer confirms anomaly zone.
3. Player chooses one burden-token to release.
4. Tone wheel stabilizes the fracture.
5. Character raises arms or opens hands.
6. Burden-token transforms into a **witness fragment**.
7. **HAL labels it: `SYMBOLIC_RELEASE`, `NOT_MEDICAL`.**
8. Player receives an integration receipt.

**Effect:** removes a fictional in-game status condition such as:
- `ECHO_WEIGHT`
- `SHAME_LOOP`
- `UNFINISHED_MEMORY`
- `SERPENT_DISTRACTION` (from `#003`)

These status conditions are **gameplay states, not psychiatric
diagnoses.** Their LNOS removal does not correspond to, simulate, or
substitute for any real-world emotional healing.
