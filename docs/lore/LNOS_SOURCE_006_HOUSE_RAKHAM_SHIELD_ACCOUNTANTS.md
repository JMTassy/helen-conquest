## 2. House Rakham (LNOS Lore)

When the old architect-king fled the Pyramid War,
he did not leave the kingdom to a family.
He left it to a **checksum**.

`RA-KAM` meant "shield key" in the cracked stone dialect:
a protective protocol,
a seal against stupid users,
a firewall disguised as inheritance law.

Over time, lazy scribes turned protocol into nobility.

The **Shield Accountants** appeared.

They claimed to own the vault.
They did not.
They owned **the paperwork around the vault**.

Their power was not gold.
Their power was access control.

They guarded doors, keys, ledgers, and extremely annoying forms.

Their sacred motto:
> *"Before enlightenment, fill Form 7B."*

## 4. Mechanic — Adept Initiate Selection (Anti-Chosen-One)

**This mechanic explicitly refuses bloodline / chosen-one / secret-destiny
framing.** Players become "adepts" by demonstrating skills, not by being
born to the right family.

Demonstrated skills:
- pattern recognition
- memory reconstruction
- ethical restraint
- refusal of false shortcuts
- ability to distinguish signal from spectacle
- ability to submit receipts before making claims

The system does not ask: *"Are you chosen?"*
