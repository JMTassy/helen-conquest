# LNOS Meta 002 — The Red Spiral Basin (Consolidated Design)

**NO CLAIM — NO SHIP — TEMPLE SANDBOX ONLY**
**Mode: SCIENCE_INSPIRED_FUNNY_MYTH — geophysics-grounded substrate +
comedic-distance design language. The mountain is not a therapist; it
is a very old stone computer with questionable grounding.**

This META file consolidates and refines the Red Spiral Basin work
distributed across `LNOS_SOURCE_010` (Tone-Locked Fracture, basin
introduction), `LNOS_SOURCE_011` (Magnetometer Probe + field-science
layer + "natural transistor" geological framing), and
`LNOS_SOURCE_012` (Outflow Release + Canyon That Exhaled), and adds
two genuinely new pieces:

1. **The Inflow Stone** — the paired counterpart to the Outflow Point.
   Where Outflow externalizes burden, Inflow grounds vision-layer
   overload.
2. **The DAN_GOBLIN Vision** — preserved as a dedicated cross-cutting
   design-commentary section, in DAN's voice, with the WUL frontmatter
   the user supplied.

The new top-level synthesis mechanic — **Canyon Breath** — pairs
Outflow and Inflow into a single named gameplay loop with a unified
HAL overlay (DATA / MYTH / SUBJECTIVE / BLOCKED CLAIM) that becomes
the visual contract for *every* sensory experience in the Basin.

`literal_claims: PARTIALLY_SEPARATED` is reused. The science substrate
is preserved (NOAA-cited Earth-field magnitudes, USGS magnetic-anomaly
maps, quartz piezoelectricity). The quarantined claims are tightened:
the trauma-shower / magnetic-healing claim has now appeared in three
source fragments and is *more* firmly quarantined here, not less.

`medical_safety_risk: HIGH` is non-negotiable. USGS guidance — that
Earth's magnetic field does not directly affect human health — remains
the anchor that prevents any drift into medical/wellness territory.

If a future reader, automated process, or downstream agent attempts
to use this file as drug-free emotional treatment endorsement, real-
world magnetic-site healing claim, paranormal proof, or to monetize
the Basin material as wellness content, the operation violates
`forbidden_use` and must be rejected.

canonical home and mark them deprecated, or keep them as the

## 5.5 Item — Goblin Magnetometer Mk.I

**The flagship field instrument of the Red Spiral Basin.** Friendly
canonical name for the Magnetometer Probe item introduced abstractly
in `#011`. The "Mk.I" suffix leaves room for upgrades in future lore.

**Description:** *a ridiculous but functional field instrument built
from a white sensing probe, copper coil, op-amps, an ADC, and too
much hope.*

**Functions:**
- detects anomaly spikes
- maps field gradients
- stabilizes Tone-Locked Fractures
- reveals Terrain Transistor output ports
- converts "vortex vibes" into inspectable signal traces

**Failure modes:**
- noise mistaken for message
- amplifier saturation
- false spiral detection
- House Rakham certification fee
- DAN licking the probe for "calibration"

### Field Probe Signal Chain (mechanic)

The seven-step gameplay loop that the Mk.I drives:

| # | Stage | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Coil Probe** | detects local changing magnetic flux |
| 2 | **Copper Winding** | converts field variation into tiny voltage (Faraday induction) |
| 3 | **Amplifier Stack** | boosts weak signal — 1–8 gain levels visible in UI |
| 4 | **Analog-to-Digital Converter** | turns voltage into readable data |
| 5 | **Signal Screen** | displays waveform, spikes, drift, spiral patterns |
| 6 | **HAL Classifier** | separates: measured signal / symbolic interpretation / unsupported claim |
| 7 | **Receipt Writer** | stores scan result as `FIELD_SCAN_RECEIPT` |

The chain is the gameplay-readable form of META_002 §9's HAL overlay:
DATA at stage 5, MYTH at stage 6 (interpretive layer), CLAIM is what
the Receipt Writer **does not** issue.

### Six signal types

Every scan classifies its signal into one of:

| Type | Source | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| `FIELD_NOISE` | ambient | nothing actionable; baseline noise |
| `FRACTURE_PULSE` | nearby Tone-Locked Fracture | proceed to tone-tuning loop |
| `STONE_SERVER_HANDSHAKE` | buried Shutu structure | dig site nearby |
| `BIRDLINE_ECHO` | migration anomaly | route signal worth following |
| `AKASHIC_BLEED` | Layer-2/3 leakage (`#008`) | grounding may be needed (Inflow Stone) |
| `HOUSE_RAKHAM_FAKE_SIGNAL` | bureaucratic interference | a permit issue, not a real anomaly |

The taxonomy is the **vocabulary of measurement** in the Basin. Every
scan returns one of these labels — never "the universe answered."

### Akashic Receiver (mode of operation)

The Mk.I, set to receive mode, is the Akashic Receiver.

**The player cannot "talk to the universe."**

The player **can** scan terrain signals and ask:
- What pattern is present?
- Is it repeatable?
- Does it correlate with tone, color, birdline, or fracture behavior?
- Can it produce an archive clue?

Patterns that satisfy all four become `ARCHIVE_CLUE` candidates. Patterns
that satisfy fewer remain measurements without canon status. Patterns
that satisfy *zero* fall into `FIELD_NOISE`.

## 6. Mechanic — Inflow Stone (**NEW**)

**The missing counterpart to the Outflow Point.**

Where Outflow externalizes burden, Inflow **grounds vision-layer
overload**. A player who has spent too long in `VISION_LAYER` (`#008`,
`#009`) — feathers vibrating, geometry destabilizing, masks proliferating —
needs a place to come down without claiming they have been "healed."

**Type:** vision-layer grounding ritual
**Location:** Inflow Stone, Red Spiral Basin

**Triggers:**
- accumulated VISION_LAYER intensity above a threshold
- destabilized self-anchor (Objecthood Collapse aftermath, `#009`)
- player explicitly selects "ground myself"
- HAL flags risk of confusing intensity with truth

**Process:**
1. Player approaches the Inflow Stone.
2. Player lies down or sits in stillness (gesture matters).
3. Magnetometer reads inward field gradient (the Stone "pulls").
4. Tone wheel matches a low, steady band (no sweep — the Inflow does
   not invite searching, it invites settling).
5. The stone pulls the noise downward as visible particles.
6. **HAL labels the event `SYMBOLIC_GROUNDING`.**
7. VISION_LAYER intensity decreases by a fixed amount.
8. The UI stops "screaming purple geometry" (per DAN_GOBLIN, §10).
9. The Feather Antenna stops vibrating. Compass stops arguing with
   itself.
10. Player can think again.

**Output:**
- VISION_LAYER intensity reduced (gameplay state, not psychiatric
  state)
- one in-game `GROUNDING_FRAGMENT` issued
- **no medical claim admitted**

The Inflow Stone is **explicitly not** a meditation guide, a wellness
practice, or therapeutic instruction. It is a gameplay state-reduction
mechanic that requires the player to choose stillness over performance
*as a game move*.

```

HAL            →  refuses healing claims

**Player loop:**

1. Reach Outflow Point. Discharge a burden token. Receive integration
   receipt.
2. Walk to Inflow Stone. Ground accumulated vision-layer intensity.
   Receive grounding fragment.

**Failure states (from DAN_GOBLIN, §10):**

  with no return key

## 8. Quest Prototypes

### 8.1 The Mountain Has Bad Wiring

**Premise:** House Rakham has issued a laminated certificate declaring
the Red Spiral Basin a "Certified Emotional Shower." HAL objects.

**Objectives:**
1. Scan the quartz layer rumor.
2. Measure anomaly points with the Goblin Magnetometer Mk.I.
3. Find the Terrain Transistor output port.
4. Use the Burden Shower ritual on one symbolic token at the Outflow
   Point.
5. Walk to the Inflow Stone. Ground accumulated vision-layer intensity.
6. **Separate measured field data from subjective release.**
7. Submit a **dual receipt**:
   - `FIELD_SCAN_RECEIPT` (data)
   - `INTEGRATION_RECEIPT` (subjective)

**Reward:**
- `TERRAIN_TRANSISTOR_MAP` unlocked
- `BURDEN_SHOWER` gesture unlocked
- `CANYON_BREATH` loop available

The whole quest pivots on Step 6: the player must consciously
distinguish what was measured from what was felt. That moment is the
gameplay equivalent of HAL discipline.

### 8.2 The Rock Has a Microphone

**Premise:** a local mystic claims the canyon can transmit messages
across dimensions. **HAL says:** *"Claim blocked. Instrument test
allowed."*

**Objectives:**
1. Assemble the Goblin Magnetometer Mk.I (per §5.5 signal chain).
2. Place the coil probe near the Outflow Point.
3. Record baseline noise.
4. Move to the Inflow Stone and repeat.
5. Amplify the signal without saturating the meter.
6. Convert analog signal to digital trace.
7. Compare trace against tone-fracture response (`#010`).
8. Submit `FIELD_SCAN_RECEIPT`.
9. **Classify any "message" as `SYMBOLIC` unless independently
   verified.**

**Reward:** `FIELD_PROBE_SIGNAL_CHAIN` unlocked.

The two quests are complementary: 8.1 attacks the **healing-claim**
failure mode (House Rakham selling the basin as therapy); 8.2 attacks
the **paranormal-comms** failure mode (the mystic selling the basin
as an interdimensional radio). Both fail modes are quarantined; both
quests teach the player to use measurement and classification as the
defense.

## 9. HAL Overlay UI (Standardized)

Every Basin sensory experience renders the **same four-panel overlay**.
This is the **interface contract** for the entire Red Spiral Basin and
should propagate to other vision-heavy zones (Akashic Atrium, Claustrum
Gate).

```
HAL CLASSIFICATION
─────────────────────────────────────────────
 DATA       :  field scan, location, anomaly delta, tone response
 MYTH       :  mountain breath, terrain transistor, burden shower
 SUBJECTIVE :  player reports release, calm, grief, laughter, reset
 BLOCKED    :  healing, trauma treatment, magnetic purification,
               scientific proof
─────────────────────────────────────────────
```

The four panels make every claim layer **simultaneously visible** to
the player. The player cannot accidentally collapse "what I felt" into
"what was measured" because they're sitting next to each other on
screen.

### 9.1 HAL Allowed / Blocked Phrasing (canonical word list)

When HAL emits in-game text near a Basin scan or ritual, it draws from
a **fixed allowed-list** and a **fixed blocked-list**. This keeps HAL's
voice consistent and prevents in-fiction drift from the file's
boundary.

**Allowed (always safe to say):**

> *Signal recorded.*
> *Pattern repeated.*
> *Anomaly detected.*
> *Tone correlation observed in-game.*
> *Subjective release recorded. No physical or medical claim admitted.*
> *Geology-inspired metaphor. Do not promote to physics.*

**Blocked (must never appear in HAL's voice in-game):**

> *The universe answered.*
> *Interdimensional communication proven.*
> *The vortex transmitted intelligence.*
> *The field heals or upgrades consciousness.*
> *Sedona is a real paranormal site.*
> *This frequency will heal you.*

The blocked list is **not** decorative. If a future writer is tempted
to put any of these phrases in HAL's mouth for tone, they have stepped
outside the file's boundary. HAL is the boundary; HAL does not narrate
through it.
