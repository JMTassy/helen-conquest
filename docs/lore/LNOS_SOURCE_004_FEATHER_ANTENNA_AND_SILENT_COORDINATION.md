## 3. UI States

The Feather Antenna communicates through six visible states. Color and
motion are the entire interface — no text, no numbers.

| State | Glyph | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| still | ⚪ | no active flow detected |
| trembling | 🔵 | weak current present |
| glowing | 🟡 | unstable route — proceed with attention |
| burning | 🔴 | harmful current — back away |
| aligned | 🟢 | safe crossing — go now |
| singing | 🟣 | group synchronization — others nearby are aligned with you |

**Design intent:** the player learns to read the feather faster than they
can read text. The state IS the message.

---

## 4. Mechanic — Silent Group Coordination

When two or more players carry Feather Antennas in the same region, the
feathers can enter the **singing** (🟣) state simultaneously. This is the
silent-coordination signal: every player in the singing state is currently
on a route the system has verified as safe.

No voice chat is required.
No text is required.
The feathers do the talking.

**Player actions:**
1. Carry the Feather Antenna openly (must be visible to other players).
2. Observe other players' feather states.
3. Move toward `aligned` (🟢) when alone, `singing` (🟣) when grouped.
4. Avoid moving when `burning` (🔴), regardless of what other players do.
5. When the whole group is `singing` (🟣), the route is collectively
   verified — proceed together.

**Failure modes:**
- A player who fakes a `singing` state (via cosmetic mods) is marked
  `UNTRUSTED` by the system on the next discrepancy with their actual
  feather state.
- A group that ignores a single `burning` (🔴) feather among them and
  proceeds anyway loses the group-synchronization bonus and may trigger a
  Serpent Hall distraction event (see #003).
