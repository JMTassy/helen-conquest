redirects.

is **not magical** — it is a fictional in-game calibration target with
no claim about real-world acoustics.

**Inputs:**

- color response

**Gameplay loop:**
1. Sweep through tones.

3. Detect the stable band.
4. Hold tone long enough to read the glyph.
5. Submit observation receipt.
6. Unlock temporary passage.

The passage is **temporary**. Sound does not unlock canon. Sound unlocks
*moments*.
